I am an PhD Candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at the Tsinghua University, advised by Carlo Vittorio Cannistraci. I obtained my Bachelor and Master degrees from Wuhan University.
My research focuses on developing brain-inspired efficient AI, including sparse training, pruning, and quantization.
